App development

Software solutions

Jive App

Jive is the ultimate party music app. Jive allows users to join local parties and create playlists with friends. The perfect app for parties and roadtrips, Jive lets friends listen to music together without being confined to a single device.

Zaky App

The Zaky App was designed for NuturedByDesign as an app that allows newborn mothers to track the growth and development of their newborn baby. The Zaky App design was meant to be used in conjunction with the Zaky sensor, a sensor that monitors stats of the baby. The app also allows mothers to log information about their baby, contact healthcare professionals, and communicate with other newbord mothers.

UT NSF Hurricane Harvey Project

As a part of the Computational Media Lab in the Journalism School at the University of Texas at Austin, the NSF Hurricane Harvey Project is a project aimed at developing a tool that utilizes machine learning in order to dynamically identify individuals in need of disaster help.
