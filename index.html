Skills

Design

Justin is pursuing a M.S. in Information Studies from the University of Texas at Austin through which he has had the opportunity to take courses in design and UX development. He enjoys designing both for mobile, web, and desktop technologies.

App development

Justin has years of experience in web app development and mobile app development. He has designed, implemented, and released apps for both Android and iOS and has developed a variety of web app solutions.

Software solutions

Justin has a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin. With experience in virtual reality development, machine learning, and blockchain develoment, he has experience creating cutting edge solutions to diverse problems.

Portfolio

Jive App

Jive is the ultimate party music app. Jive allows users to join local parties and create playlists with friends. The perfect app for parties and roadtrips, Jive lets friends listen to music together without being confined to a single device.

Zaky App

The Zaky App was designed for NuturedByDesign as an app that allows newborn mothers to track the growth and development of their newborn baby. The Zaky App design was meant to be used in conjunction with the Zaky sensor, a sensor that monitors stats of the baby. The app also allows mothers to log information about their baby, contact healthcare professionals, and communicate with other newbord mothers.

NSF Hurricane Harvey Project

As a part of the Computational Media Lab in the Journalism School at the University of Texas at Austin, the NSF Hurricane Harvey Project is a project aimed at developing a tool that utilizes machine learning in order to dynamically identify individuals in need of disaster help.

Immersive Journalism

As a collaborative effort between the UT School of Journalism, journalists at the New York Times and the Washington Post, and researchers at the Texas Advanced Computing Center Visualization Lab, Immersive Journalism was designed and implemented as an intuitive application that enables journalists to create captivating virtual reality stories as 360Â° panoramic scenes.
